**Title:** The Gas Station Coffee
**Replay Line:** The coffee’s burning in the plastic cup.

A half-eaten donut on the dash,
The radio is static and a wish.
You said, "I think we’re running out of luck,"
While the sun was rising like a threat.
The coffee’s burning in the plastic cup,
I’m swallowing the bitter with the rest.
(Bridge Pivot - Perspective Shift)
You’re probably halfway to the coast by now,
Thinking that you left it all behind.
But I’m the one who’s sitting in the dark,
Realizing that the road is just a line.
