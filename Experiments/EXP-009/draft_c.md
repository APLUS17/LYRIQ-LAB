# EXP-009 — Draft C (Alternative Angle)
**SOP:** 05_The_Hook_Factory (Method 8: Loaded Question)
**Title:** Did You Even Try?
**Replay Line:** Tell me, did you even try?

**Verse 1**
The boxes sitting by the kitchen sink,
The empty spaces where the photos hung.
I’m tracing circles in the layer of dust,
Watching shadows stretch across the floor.

**Chorus**
Tell me, did you even try to stay?
Or was the exit always in your eyes?
Tell me, did you even try today?
Or did you give up on the first surprise?

**Verse 2 (Pivot - Perspective Shift)**
You’re acting like the choice was made for us,
Like some collision that we couldn't miss.
But I saw you pulling back the day we met,
Setting up the end of every kiss.
