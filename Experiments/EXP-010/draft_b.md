# EXP-010 — Draft B (Master SOP)
**SOP:** 06_The_4C_Framework (on Final Master Draft)
**Title:** The Ghost in the Spare Room
**Replay Line:** I’m living with the ghost in the spare room.

**Verse 1**
The door is sticking on the bottom hinge,
A stack of mail is yellowing in the hall.
You said, "I think we’re haunted by the past,"
While the rain was drumming on the glass.

**Chorus**
I’m living with the ghost in the spare room,
The only part of you that didn't leave.
I’m living with the ghost in the spare room,
And finally I’ve learned how to believe.

**Bridge (Pivot)**
The house is quiet for a thousand years,
The street is empty and the sky is grey.
But still I hear you walking in the night,
Waiting for the sun to take the day.
