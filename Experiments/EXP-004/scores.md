## Critic Notes

### Draft A
- **Imagery:** "Ceiling fan spinning slow and loud," "moonlight makes a pattern," and "pillows drift away" are solid, though slightly more conventional than EXP-003. **0.78**
- **Flow:** The Pulse Check had a massive impact. Every line scans almost perfectly in 4/4 time. "I used to keep the left side clear for you" (10) and "The morning’s just a shadow of the night" (9) hit the stress points exactly on the beat. **0.85**
- **Hook:** "I’m sleeping in the middle of the bed" is a classic, relatable hook concept. Very singable. **0.80**
- **Structure:** Clear setup and payoff. The final line provides a clean resolution. **0.78**

### Draft B
- **Imagery:** Trimming "We're watching" (actually "The" in this draft) and sharpening "ghost" to "don't run into ghosts" improved the punchiness. **0.82**
- **Flow:** Draft B achieves a near-perfect flow. Syllables are tight (9/8/10/9 in V1). "The only place I don't run into ghosts" (9) is a metrical upgrade over the raw version. **0.90**
- **Hook:** Repetition of the Replay Line in the chorus solidifies the hook. **0.82**
- **Structure:** 4C pass created a tighter arc. **0.82**

### Draft C
- **Imagery:** The Contradiction Mirror created very sharp imagery pairs ("house always quiet" vs "hear the kettle whistle"). **0.80**
- **Flow:** The repetitive structure (I hate/I love, I miss/I like) creates a very strong, rhythmic pulse. It's almost impossible to read this out of time. **0.88**
- **Hook:** "I miss you but I finally like the space" is a strong emotional thesis. Very catchy. **0.85**
- **Structure:** The V1 setup leads perfectly into the chorus realization. **0.80**

## Hypothesis Verdict

**CONFIRMED.** Flow average rose from 0.75 to 0.87 (Draft A 0.70 -> 0.85). The Pulse Check is the most effective Flow mutation to date. It eliminates the "tripping" lines that occurred in previous experiments when information density overrode meter.

**Secondary finding:** Contradiction Mirror (Draft C) is a powerful tool for maintaining Flow because the balanced sentence structure naturally creates balanced meter.
