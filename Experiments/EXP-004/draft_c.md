# EXP-004 — Draft C (Alternative Angle)
**SOP:** 04_The_Fracture_Lab (Method 2: Contradiction Mirror)
**Title:** I Miss You But I Like The Space
**Replay Line:** I miss you but I finally like the space.

**Verse 1**
I hate the way the house is always quiet,
I love that I can hear the kettle whistle.
I miss the way you’d take up all the room,
I like that I don't have to move the chair.

**Chorus**
I miss you but I finally like the space,
The freedom that I never wanted then.
I miss you but I finally like the space,
I’m learning how to breathe on my own again.

**Verse 2**
I’m looking for your jacket in the hall,
I’m glad I moved the boxes to the attic.
I’m holding onto everything we were,
I’m letting go of everything we weren't.
