**Title:** The Neighbor’s Porch Light
**Replay Line:** I’m watching from the neighbor’s porch light.

The street is dark except for number ten,
A yellow rectangle against the grass.
I’m sitting where the shadows meet the wood,
Watching for the way you lock the door.
I’m watching from the neighbor’s porch light,
The only part of home I still can see.
(Bridge Pivot - Leap Forward)
The winter came and buried every track,
The house is sold, the light is always off.
But still I find the version of myself,
Sitting on the step in eighty-nine.
