# EXP-011 — Draft C (Alternative Angle)
**SOP:** 05_The_Hook_Factory (Method 6: Escape Plan)
**Title:** I Never Liked the Coffee
**Replay Line:** I never actually liked the coffee.

**Verse 1**
A chipped mug sitting on the coaster now,
The steam is rising like a hollow ghost.
I drank it every morning for a year,
Just to watch you smiling at the stove.

**Chorus**
I never actually liked the coffee,
I just liked the way you held the spoon.
I never actually liked the coffee,
I just liked being in the room.

**Verse 2 (Pivot - Perspective Shift)**
You’re buying different beans in Tennessee,
Thinking that I miss the bitter taste.
But I’m the one who’s pouring out the pot,
And finally cleaning up the waste.

**Outro (Twist)**
I never actually liked the coffee,
And I never actually liked you.
