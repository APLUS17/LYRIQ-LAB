# EXP-011 — Draft B (4C Pass)
**SOP:** 06_The_4C_Framework (applied to Draft A)
**North Star Line:** I’m reaching for the spare key.

**Verse 1**
Rusted washer and a soggy leaf,
Metal glints against the black cement.
I swore I’d never crawl back to your porch,
But hunger drags me through the garden gate.

**Chorus**
I’m reaching for the spare key,
The only part of us I didn't lose.
I’m reaching for the spare key,
Trying to remember how to choose.

**Bridge (Pivot)**
The lock is changed, the porch is painted white,
A stranger’s boots are sitting by the door.
But still I feel the cold against my palm,
As if the metal still belonged to me.

**Outro (Twist)**
I’m reaching for the spare key,
But I’m the one who locked you out.
