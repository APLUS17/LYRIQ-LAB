# EXP-006 — Draft B (4C Pass)
**SOP:** 06_The_4C_Framework (applied to Draft A)
**North Star Line:** I’m holding onto the broken snow globe.

**Verse 1**
A plastic Eiffel Tower on the floor,
Water making circles on the rug.
You said, "I think we’re better when we’re lost,"
Leaving all the glitter in my hand.

**Chorus**
I’m holding onto the broken snow globe,
Where the glitter never settles anymore.
I’m holding onto the broken snow globe,
Trying to be better than we were.

**Bridge (Pivot)**
Ten years of dust are sitting on the shelf,
I found a different way to be alone.
But every time I hear the sound of glass,
I’m back inside the hallway on the rug.
