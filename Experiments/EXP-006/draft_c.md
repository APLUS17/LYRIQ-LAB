# EXP-006 — Draft C (Alternative Angle)
**SOP:** 03_The_Feeling_Machine (Method 4: 5 Senses)
**Title:** The Cold Side of the Pillow
**Replay Line:** I’m looking for the cold side of the pillow.

**Verse 1**
The scent of lavender and laundry soap,
The rough texture of the linen on my face.
The sound of tires on the wet asphalt,
Reminds me that the driveway is still empty.

**Chorus**
I’m looking for the cold side of the pillow,
The only part of you that’s left to find.
I’m looking for the cold side of the pillow,
To keep the heat from burning up my mind.

**Verse 2 (Pivot - Perspective Shift)**
You’re probably sleeping in a different city,
Under sheets that haven't learned your name.
Do you ever reach across the mattress,
And realize that the feeling’s not the same?
