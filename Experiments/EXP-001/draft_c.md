# EXP-001 / Draft C — Alternative Direction

**SOP:** 02_The_Spark_Engine
**Method:** Title First → "Said Enough"
**Angle:** Same emotional territory but from the *other person's* perspective — the one who received the honest text. Different angle on the same trigger.

---

## Verse 1

You said the thing you'd been saving
three weeks of nothing, then that
I read it twice in the parking lot
put my phone away, drove back

I always knew you were careful
the way you chose your words like they cost you
I just didn't know what it was costing
until you finally said enough

## Pre-Chorus

and now I'm standing in your honesty
like a room I didn't know was small

## Chorus

you said enough
and I heard everything that came before it
you said enough
and I realized how long I'd been ignoring it
wasn't the words that broke it
it was all the years you held them back
you said enough
and I can't unhear that

## Verse 2

I called my sister, didn't explain it
just sat on the phone while she talked
she asked if I was eating properly
I said yeah, I've been going for walks

I keep replaying the timestamp
2:47 in the afternoon
like if I know exactly when it happened
I can figure out what I'm supposed to do

## Bridge

you were trying to be kind by staying quiet
I was trying to be easy to love
somewhere in between we made a silence
and called it enough
