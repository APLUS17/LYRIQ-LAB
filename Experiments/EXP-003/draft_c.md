# EXP-003 — Draft C (Alternative Angle)
**SOP:** 05_The_Hook_Factory (Method 6: Escape Plan)
**Title:** I'm Not Sorry For The Silence
**Replay Line:** Silence is the only thing I've kept.

**Verse 1**
You're waiting for the text that never comes,
The phone is buzzing in the empty drawer.
I'm counting cracks along the plaster ceiling,
To keep from naming what I'm feeling now.

**Chorus**
Silence is the only thing I've kept,
The only promise I have never broke.
You want the truth, I want the heavy peace,
Of knowing that the talking finally ceased.

**Verse 2**
I used to fill the air with hollow words,
To bridge the gap between your heart and mine.
But now I let the quiet take its place,
And watch the color leaving from your face.
