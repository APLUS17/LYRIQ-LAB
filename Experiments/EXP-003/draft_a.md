**Title:** The Last Time I Meant It
**Replay Line:** I said I'm staying, and I meant it.

The keys are heavy in the porcelain bowl,
We’re watching shadows climb the kitchen wall.
A half-full glass of water on the sill,
A clock that's tired of holding up the day.
I said I’m staying, and I meant it then,
Before the winter leaked into the room.
Now I'm folding up the map we never used,
To find the version of me that was true.
I said I'm staying, and I meant it then,
But I'm leaving with the things I can't undo.
